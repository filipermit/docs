### How to filter specific logs?

You can filter audit logs based on **user**, **date** or **decision**.

![Audit Log permitted hover Screenshot](/img/updated/audit-log/audit-log-2.png)

You may also filter according to **time**. To filter by time you can use the **date filter box** on the left and choose the desired time span.

![Audit Log permitted hover Screenshot](/img/updated/audit-log/audit-log-3.png)

Below you can see that we filtered all the `permitted` decisions on the `folder` resource that were made yesterday.
